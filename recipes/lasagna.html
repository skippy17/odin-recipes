Yum yum Lasagna

Description

It's lasagna. Garfield likes it a lot. Effectively pasta cake.

Ingredients

  • Lasanga noodles
  • Sauce
  • Chopped meat
  • Ricotta
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Brown the meat with salt and pepper. Drain it.
  3. In your lasagna pan, put layers down until you run out.
